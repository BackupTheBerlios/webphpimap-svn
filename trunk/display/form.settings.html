IMAP Host
URL or IP of your IMAP-Host
IMAP Port
Port of your IMAP-Host, usually 143
Username
This is the Username of your IMAP Account and will be your Login for phPIMap
Password (phPIMap)
This is the Password of your IMAP Account. Attention: This password will be encrypted.
Password (IMAP)
This is the Password of your IMAP Account. Attention: This password can not be encrypted due to technical reasons. If you leave this blank your Password for phPIMap-Login may be used.
Todo IMAP Resource
IMAP Folder(s) containing Todos. Seperate them with spaces.
Calendar IMAP Resource
IMAP Folder(s) containing Events. Seperate them with spaces.
Contact IMAP Resource
IMAP Folder(s) containing Contacts. Seperate them with spaces.
